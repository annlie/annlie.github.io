j.a.w cooper

J.A.W Cooper is an illustrator and art director. Her pronouns are she/her/they/them. She was born in England but has lived all around the world because of her parents' job as freshwater ecologists. Some of the places she has resided in are Kenya, Sweden, Ireland, and California. She currently keeps herself busy as a freelance artist for various advertisements, character designs, sketches, and storyboards.



"My name can confuse people; 'J.A.W.'' is an acronym of my first three names but since I was a young child I have introduced myself simply by my last name 'Cooper' which suits me much better."
