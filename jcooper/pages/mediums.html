art mediums

Cooper has used a variety of art mediums in her years of creating: prisma colors, brush pens, copic markers, graphite pencils, gouche, ink, bronze, polymer clay as well as using Photoshop and Procreate for her digital artwork.


This sculpture is one of two sculptures she has created so far, both being of a Sphynx cat. Her other sculpture was made out of polymer clay while this was made out of bronze.
